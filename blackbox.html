intro paragraph

In my 9th grade new media and tech class we were assigned to try and guess what's in the “mystery black box”. The mystery black box is a mysterious box that is locked and our teacher Mr sinusass gave us a challenge to try and guess what it is .I believe the mystery black box is a bunch of tubes that hold water which explains why sometimes you get less and sometimes more . I believe in that in the black box there is a bunch of tubes because after i did 50 trials each time about 1000 ML and 30 out of the 50 i got lose and the other 15 i got more and the last 5 i got the same amount i poured in sometimes i would get less water and sometimes i would get more water and sometimes the same i believe water might be getting stuck so that explains why sometimes you get more and sometimes less and we made a model with tubes which gave kind of the same results which helps my claim that it is tubes.

claim

Models can be useful because you can compare results that you got from your model with the results you get from the black box. I created my own box using cardboard containers, tubes, funnels and trays and we did some trials and got identical results as the mystery blackbox. My argument is that the black box has tubes and I provided evidence that is pretty convincing after putting water in the black box and after putting it in my own model I got the same results.
